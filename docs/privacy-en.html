Klip Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026 · 简体中文

Klip (“the App”) is developed by Cai Xinyun. It is a macOS menu-bar clipboard history manager.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, upload, or share your personal data with any third party. There is no networking, no analytics SDK, and no crash-reporting service.

Data Stored Locally

  • Clipboard history: Text, images, and file references you copy are stored only on your Mac for history and search.
  • Database encryption: The history database is encrypted with SQLCipher; the key is stored in the system Keychain.
  • Preferences: Settings such as hotkeys, retention rules, and excluded apps are stored in UserDefaults.
  • Image files: Copied images are saved as files under Application Support on your Mac.

All of the above stays on your device and never leaves your Mac.

Permissions

The App runs in the macOS sandbox and does not request Accessibility, network, location, or contacts access. Pasting uses copy-only mode: content is written to the system clipboard and focus returns to your previous app; you press ⌘V to paste.

Your Control

  • Pause recording anytime in Settings → Privacy.
  • Exclude specific apps from being recorded.
  • Clear history at any time (pinned items can be kept).
  • Uninstalling the App removes all related local data.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page when this policy changes.
